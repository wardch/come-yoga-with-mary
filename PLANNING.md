# Mary Duffy - Project Plan

## Context

The website is becoming Mary Duffy's creator and educator homepage. The goal is to convert attention from Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and other social platforms into an owned email list, then grow toward workshops, mini series, playlists, workplace sessions, and other paid offers.

## Version 1 Scope

- Single conversion-focused homepage
- Hero with Mary-first positioning
- Email capture for the Burnout Recovery Reset
- Credibility section using real credentials and external sources
- Four content pillars
- Future-offer section
- Social follow links
- Person-focused SEO metadata and structured data
- Static success page for local signup flow
- Kit-ready form wiring placeholder

## Email Strategy

Use Kit for email capture, tags, automation, and ongoing weekly emails.

- Lead magnet sequence: five short emails, delivered immediately across weekdays after signup
- Weekly email: Tuesday at 8:30am Europe/Dublin until real engagement data suggests a better time
- Initial tag: `burnout-recovery-reset`

## Offer Ladder

1. Free social content, Burnout Recovery Reset, and weekly email
2. Low-cost workshop
3. Four-week mini series
4. Workplace sessions for teams and managers
5. Premium cohort, retreat, or consulting offers later
